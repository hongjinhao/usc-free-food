Join the Resident Leadership Initiative for a vibrant Lunar New Year celebration honoring culture, community, and new beginnings. Residents are invited to enjoy a variety of Asian snacks and meals, take part in hands-on activities like lantern decorating and temporary tattoos or henna, and explore the meaning behind their Lunar Zodiac sign. Come connect with fellow residents, learn something new, and celebrate the start of the Lunar New Year together in a festive and welcoming space.

This event is open to USC Housing Residents only. Residential Education operates its programs in accordance with USC's Notice of Non-Discrimination.
